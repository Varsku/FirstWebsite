HISTORY

Everslave was founded in Lohja, Finland in late 2008 and got its first shape in early 2012.
In July of that year, the band released their first demo, called 'Valediction'.
The band played gigs mainly in their hometown or somewhere nearby and their second demo-CD
'Hellride' came out in May 2013. In November 2013 singer/guitarist Alpo and drummer Janne were asked to leave the band
for various reasons. 1st of July 2014 the band announced that they've found a new vocalist,
Tanja Härkönen. Sami Rehmonen joined the band as their new drummer in January 2015. Everslave finished recordings of its 3rd demo-EP 'I Am King' in June 2015. Recordings took place at Sonic Pump Studios in Helsinki, Finland. The EP contains four songs and it's the very first recording with our singer Tanja and drummer Sami.
